```anki
> [!note] Definición
> A function with period $L$ can be written as a __Fourier Series__:
===
$$
\begin{gather}
    f(x) = \frac{1}{2} a_0 + ∑\limits_{n=1}^∞ a_n \cos(k_n x) + ∑\limits_{n=1}^∞ b_n \sin(k_n x)\\
    \\
    \quad \text{Where } {k_n = \frac{nπx}{L}}
\end{gather}
$$
```
